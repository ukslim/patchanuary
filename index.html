Patchanuary

Patchanuary has a minimum viable website.

Inspired by projects such as Inktober, the idea is to create a sound inspired by a prompt word, each day of January.

The sound could be a one-shot sound effect, a playable synth patch, a melody, a long ambient piece, or whatever else you want. One way to create the sounds might be to configure a synthesiser patch - but there are no rules to say that's the only way.

One approach to Patchanuary is to share each day's sound on social media - why not use the hashtag #patchanuary - but of course you are free to do whatever you like.
